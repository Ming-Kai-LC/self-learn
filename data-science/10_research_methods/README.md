# Research Methods

Advanced statistical analysis and research skills for data science.

## Overview

Research methods provide the rigorous statistical foundation and experimental design skills needed for high-quality data science work. These skills are essential for causal inference, A/B testing, and publishable research.

## Projects in This Domain

1. **data-science-research-skills** (~10 hours)
   - Research methodology for data science
   - Reproducibility and documentation
   - Experimental design
   - Literature review and paper reading
   - Technical writing

2. **data-science-research-methods-advanced** (~15 hours)
   - Advanced statistical methods
   - Causal inference techniques
   - Time series analysis
   - Bayesian statistics
   - Research applications
