## Common Challenges at A2

### Challenge 1: Choosing the Right Tense
**Solution:** Focus on the PURPOSE of what you're saying
- Past continuous: Describe the SCENE, BACKGROUND, or DURATION
- Past simple: Tell what HAPPENED, the MAIN EVENTS
- Present perfect: Connect PAST to PRESENT, or talk about EXPERIENCES

### Challenge 2: Form Overload
**Solution:** Practice one form at a time
- Master affirmative first
- Then negative
- Then questions
- Finally, mix them

### Challenge 3: Spelling -ing Forms
**Solution:** Learn the 4 spelling rules
1. Most verbs: just add -ing (work â†’ working)
2. Verbs ending in -e: drop -e, add -ing (make â†’ making)
3. One-syllable verbs with vowel + consonant: double final consonant (run â†’ running)
4. Verbs ending in -ie: change to -y (lie â†’ lying)
