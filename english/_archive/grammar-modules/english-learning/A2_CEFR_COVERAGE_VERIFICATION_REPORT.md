## 1. CEFR A2 Requirements Overview

Based on Cambridge English Profile, English Grammar Profile research, and CEFR descriptors:

### A2 Criterial Features (Must-Have)

1. **Consistent Past Simple use** with extensive irregular verbs (200+)
2. **Future reference** with going to, will, and present continuous
3. **Basic modals:** can, should, must, have to, may, might, could, would like
4. **Comparatives and superlatives** (-er/-est, more/most, irregular forms)
5. **Past Continuous** for interrupted actions and background
6. **Present Perfect introduction** for life experiences (ever/never)
7. **Zero and First Conditionals** for general truths and real future possibilities
8. **Time clauses:** when, while, before, after, as soon as, until
9. **Basic quantifiers:** some/any, much/many, a lot of, (a) few/(a) little
10. **Adverbs** of manner and degree
11. **Basic relative clauses:** who, which, that (introduction)
12. **Because clauses** for giving reasons
13. **Verb patterns:** infinitive vs gerund basics
14. **Pronouns:** indefinite and basic reflexive
15. **Phrasal verbs:** common two-particle verbs (introduction)

### A2 Mastery Indicators

- Reliable past tense use
- Future reference with going to/will
- Comparative descriptions
- Modal verbs for advice, obligation, possibility
- Basic question formation across tenses
- 1,000-1,500 active vocabulary
- Handle routine communication exchanges
- Errors frequent but rarely impede communication

**Assessment Benchmarks:** Cambridge KET pass (140+), IELTS Band 3-4, TOEFL Basic level
