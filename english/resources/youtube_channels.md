## Learning Strategy by Level

### A1-A2: Foundation Building

**Daily routine (20 min)**:
1. Watch one BBC Learning English video (5 min)
2. Write down 5 new words
3. Repeat key sentences aloud
4. Practice with ChatGPT using new vocabulary

**Weekly focus**: One channel, 3-4 videos maximum

### B1-B2: Skill Development

**Daily routine (30 min)**:
1. Watch one instructional video (Lucy, Emma, Rachel)
2. Take notes on key points
3. Practice exercises if provided
4. Discuss topic with AI

**Weekly focus**: Mix of teaching channels + 1-2 content videos for challenge

### C1-C2: Immersion & Mastery

**Daily routine (40-60 min)**:
1. Watch native content (TED, Vox, news)
2. Summarize main points in writing
3. Extract advanced vocabulary/expressions
4. Discuss and debate with AI

**Weekly focus**: 80% native content, 20% learning channels for fine-tuning

## How to Watch Effectively

### Active Watching (Recommended)

1. **First viewing**: English subtitles ON, take notes
2. **Pause frequently**: Write down useful phrases
3. **Repeat sections**: Difficult parts 2-3 times
4. **Shadow speak**: Repeat after the speaker
5. **Post-watch**: Discuss content with AI

### Passive Watching (Supplemental)

- Background listening while cooking/cleaning
- Builds familiarity with rhythm and intonation
- Less learning, more exposure

## Integration with AI Practice

After watching any video:

### ChatGPT/Claude Prompts

**Comprehension check**:
```
I just watched a YouTube video about [topic]. Here's what I understood: [summary]

Please:
1. Check my summary for accuracy
2. Correct any grammar mistakes
3. Ask me 3 questions to test my understanding
4. Suggest related vocabulary I should learn
```

**Pronunciation practice**:
```
I'm watching Rachel's English videos on [sound]. Can you:
1. Explain when this sound appears in words
2. Give me 20 practice words with this sound
3. Create 5 sentences I can practice
4. Test my understanding with a quiz
```

**Vocabulary expansion**:
```
From today's video, I learned these 5 words: [list]

Please:
1. Check if I understood their meanings correctly
2. Give me example sentences for each
3. Suggest 5 related words I should know
4. Create a fill-in-the-blank exercise
```

- Understand 80%+ of Business English Pod

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Passive watching only**: Always take notes, practice actively

❌ **Staying in comfort zone**: Push to harder content regularly

❌ **Not using subtitles strategically**: Use them, then remove them

❌ **Watching without follow-up**: Always practice with AI afterwards

❌ **Too many channels**: Focus on 3-5 channels maximum

✅ **Do this instead**: Active watching + AI practice + gradual challenge increase
