### Business Overview

**Core Business**: Underground utilities engineering specialist

**Main Services**:
1. **Horizontal Directional Drilling (HDD)**
   - Non-invasive method for laying underground pipes
   - Minimal surface disruption
   - Used for utilities (water, gas, telecommunications, electricity)

2. **Open-Cut Excavation**
   - Traditional trenching method
   - Used where HDD is not feasible

3. **Micro-Trenching**
   - Narrow trench excavation for fiber optic cables
   - Growing demand with 5G rollout

**Business Model**:
- Project-based revenue (contract wins from government and private sector)
- Potential for recurring revenue from maintenance contracts
- Capital intensive (requires specialized drilling equipment)

### Unit Economics (To be calculated)

**Key Metrics for Café Business**:
- **Revenue per outlet**: Total revenue / Number of outlets
- **Same-store sales growth (SSSG)**: YoY growth for existing outlets
- **Average transaction value (ATV)**: Revenue / Number of transactions
- **Customer frequency**: Transactions / Unique customers
- **Food cost %**: Food costs / Revenue (benchmark: 25-35%)
- **Labor cost %**: Labor costs / Revenue (benchmark: 25-35%)
- **Prime cost**: Food cost % + Labor cost % (benchmark: <60%)
- **Rent as % of sales**: Rent / Revenue (benchmark: <10%)
- **EBITDA per outlet**: Outlet EBITDA / Number of outlets
- **Payback period**: Investment per outlet / Annual cash flow per outlet
